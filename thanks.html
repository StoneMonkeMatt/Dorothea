Acknowledgements & Thanks

A personal note of gratitude from the Estate Manager

I would like to express my sincere thanks to the following organisations and individuals for their ongoing support and commitment to Dorothea Quarry:

  • Dorothea Pumped Hydro: For their stewardship in owning and maintaining the quarry site.
  • CADW: For their vital contribution to the upkeep and preservation of our heritage structures.
  • Bangor University: For their continued archaeological interest and research into the site's rich history.
  • Talysarn Community Council: For their support and collaboration in community matters.
  • The Local Residents: Who independently and tirelessly work to keep the quarry litter-free and respected.

This site is a shared heritage, and its preservation is only possible through this collective effort.

Matt Shaw
Estate Manager

Cydnabyddiaeth a Diolch

Nodyn personol o ddiolch gan Reolwr yr Ystad

Hoffwn fynegi fy niolch diffuant i'r sefydliadau ac unigolion canlynol am eu cefnogaeth a'u hymrwymiad parhaus i Chwarel Dorothea:

  • Dorothea Pumped Hydro: Am eu stiwardiaeth wrth berchenogi a chynnal safle'r chwarel.
  • CADW: Am eu cyfraniad hanfodol i waith cynnal a chadw a diogelu ein strwythurau treftadaeth.
  • Prifysgol Bangor: Am eu diddordeb archeolegol parhaus ac ymchwil i hanes cyfoethog y safle.
  • Cyngor Cymuned Talysarn: Am eu cefnogaeth a'u cydweithrediad mewn materion cymunedol.
  • Y Preswylwyr Lleol: Sy'n gweithio'n annibynnol ac yn ddiflino i gadw'r chwarel yn rhydd o sbwriel ac yn barchus.

Mae'r safle hwn yn dreftadaeth a rennir, ac nid yw ei ddiogelu ond yn bosibl trwy'r ymdrech ar y cyd hon.

Matt Shaw
Rheolwr yr Ystad
